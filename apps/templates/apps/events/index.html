Join us for an exhilarating 48-hour journey into the future of AI. Vibranta challenges you to push the boundaries of prompt engineering and create groundbreaking AI applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?
Anyone with a passion for AI and prompt engineering, regardless of experience level! We welcome students, professionals, and enthusiasts from all backgrounds.
What do I need to participate?
A computer with internet access, your favorite coding environment, and a creative mind ready to tackle AI challenges!
Is there a registration fee?
No, participation in Vibranta is completely free of charge.
Can I participate as part of a team?
Yes, you can participate individually or in teams of up to 4 members.
